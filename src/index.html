Features

Share or embed a speech anywhere on the internet
Every piece of Parlameter content can be pointed to directly and shared with a permalink or embedded on other websites by clicking the icons below each information card.
Quote a part of the speech
Just like other pieces of Parlameter content, every single sentence from your transcripts can live its own independent internet life.
Live topic streams
Search for any keyword and see all of its occurences in the corpus. As time passes, the embedded card will update, acting as a live topic stream from your sessions.
Automated email alerts
Let us do media clipping for you! Allow people to subscribe to custom keyword triggers and receive an email every time those trigger words are mentioned.
Interactive results of a single voting event
The card with most embeds on every Parlameter installation. It makes it easy for your audience to understand how individuals voted and what might be interesting about a particular vote.
AI detection of unexpected voting behaviour
Never send a man to do the machine’s job. Our AI automatically labels ballots that deviate from previous voting dynamics of the council members.
Vote comparator
Brings your audience answers to specific questions like "when did members of one group vote the same as members of their biggest rival group?"
